WHO IS COLE SOLOMON?


Cole is a UX Engineer showing versatility as an Interaction designer by day, mobile developer by night- call him Bruce Wayne (without the mask of course!). I love to add value to brands by demystifying ideas from the concept stage and produce working prototypes or MVP, whether they be related to user research, interface design, web design, or mobile development.

I am exploring my skills currently as an Interaction Designer with Divercity.io, USA, and as a Technical lead with Antigravity Tech Hub, USA. (Yes, Bruce Wayne again)

When I am not in front of my MacBook, speaking, or organizing tech conferences, then you can find me playing video games or at a sporting center supporting the best team in the world; Barcelona.

Right now, I am dabbling into the MERN Stack, the Web is catching my fancy, as learning and relearning is a skill that I value.
